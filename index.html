About

Animation “Studio Velocipede” is a Georgian start-up that produces and promotes educational animations for children (preschoolers) in Georgian language. The Studio was established in 2017 The very first project of the Studio is educational animation series Giraffe Jozee. Happy Giraffe Jozee is kid’s friend and their guide on their way of early learning and development. Giraffe Jozee series gained nationwide popularity in a country of Georgia. Currently the content is being translated into English and Spanish languages with the purpose of making Jozee the most popular giraffe around the world.
